HOW TO MAKE PAPER FLOWERS


In the following guide I will do my best to teach you how to make craft paper flowers. These easy, yet beautiful creations are only limited to your imagination. You can mix and match colors, shapes and sizes to really make an impression. They are great for gifts on a loved ones birthday, perhaps mothers day, or on any special occassion needing a personal touch.

  • Scissors ·
  • Pencil or Paintbrush ·
  • Elmers Glue

  • Squares of Paper(equal dimensions)


First Steps


You will be using four squares of paper.
First, fold a square in half diagonally (as shown in the picture above.)
Next fold it again in half, and then again one more time.
(This should make for a total of three folds)
Do this to each of the remaining three squares of paper.
Back to Top


Second Steps


First, Draw a line from the inner crease of the folded square to the bottom.
This line will determine the shape of the petal.
Second, cut out the line you just drew.
Third, cut the opposite tip of the paper, a straight cut across.
Lastly, open the folded petal to see if your satisfied with the outcome.
(If not, re-fold and make adjustments to your liking.)
Back to Top


Third Steps


First, take one of the whole petals and cut out one section of of it.
Second, take another petal and cut out two pieces of that petal.
Third, take the next petal and cut out three pieces of the petal.
Lastly, take the last petal and cut it in half (four pieces of the petal).
Back to Top


Fourth Steps


In this step take your pencil (or whatever "rolling" tool of your choice) and curl each end of the petal.
Repeat this for every petal you have.
You should be curling eight petals total.
Back to Top


Fifth Steps


First, place a line of glue on one side of the open end of the petal piece.
Second, attach the two open ends of the petal together, "closing" the petal.
Repeat this step for each petal piece you have.
Again, this should be a total of eight.
Back to Top


Sixth Steps


First, place a ring of glue along the bottom of the next to largest petal you have.
Second, place this petal inside of the largest petal, making sure to press them together so they stick.
Wait for it to dry.
Next, grab the next smallest petal and place glue along it's bottom.
Then place this petal inside of the other two already glued together pieces and press it down.
Repeat this step until all pieces are glued together.
