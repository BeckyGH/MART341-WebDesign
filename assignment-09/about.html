THE STORY BEHIND THE CRAFT

These paper flowers started as a way for my daughter and I to give a gift that would be thoughtful and last a long time, or at least longer than fresh ones. We were brainstorming what our friends and family would enjoy recieving and at the same time finding something that we would enjoy making. After thinking about it for awhile and surfing the web, we decided that paper flowers were a good bet.

With this craft project you are only limited to your imagination when it comes to styles, sizes, and combinations. They can be made into a lovely bouquet or given solo as a token to show your love.
Not to mention they are super easy and fun to make!

As with all craft projects you can find numerous tutorials on youtube, and pinterest.
Go check em out and happy crafting!
